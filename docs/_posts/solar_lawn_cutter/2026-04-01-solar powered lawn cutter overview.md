### Goal
lawn mower (cutter) that is solar powered, lives on the lawn, always cutting.

### System
* solar powered
* scissors for grass cutting

### Rationale
*Solar:* to make a simple robot that can be placed on the lawn with no further setup/infrastructure. Does not require charging dock installation.

*Cutting with scissors:* Aiming for efficiency, low noise, ability to cut relatively high grass. Seems impossibly slow, but if the robot is always cutting, does it need to cut fast??
