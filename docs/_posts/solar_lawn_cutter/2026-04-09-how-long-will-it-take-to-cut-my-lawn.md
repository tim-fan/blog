title: How long will it take to cut my lawn?
date: 2026-04-06 12:30:00 +0000

Lawn is 20m^2

Scissors are cutting every 3s, covering 5 square centimeters?

20 / (0.0005/3)/60/60 = 33.3 hrs

 If it can run for 4.7 hours a day, it could cut the full lawn in a week **assuming** perfect navigation (robot never covers the same area twice).

Seems close enough to viable.

Areas for optimization:
* smart navigation for efficient coverage - localization system?
* local sensing of long grass 
* faster cutting (while maintaining low noise)
