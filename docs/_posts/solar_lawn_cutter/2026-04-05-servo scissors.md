Swapped scissor actuator from brushed DC motor to servo. Advantages:
* only rotates 180 degrees - saves space
* acceptably quiet
* driving back and forth means scissors don't jam

I'm now happy with the cutting mechanism, will focus on wheels to get reliable traversal across lawn, then will think about navigation.
